# WorldPool

> High-performance actor pooling with spatial activation and replication.

WorldPool provides a spatial actor pooling system that streams actors in and out of shared pools based on zone proximity. Delta persistence ensures actors retain their state between pool acquisitions, and replication support keeps clients synchronized without per-actor bandwidth overhead.
